"All-Green" Behavioral Fraud Detection
The Problem: In 2026, "Industrialized Deception" means fraudsters use AI to manipulate legitimate users into making transfers. Traditional checks show "All Green" because the user is genuinely authenticated, but they are acting under duress or manipulation (e.g., Pig Butchering scams).

Velo-stream Role: Monitor sub-millisecond behavioral signals (hesitation during transfer, unusual session length, simultaneous device access).

Velo-agent Role: A Behavioral Persona that evaluates these signals against a "Baseline of Normality." If a user suddenly transfers their life savings to a new account while showing "hesitation signals," the agent triggers a "Soft Intervention" (e.g., an AI-led voice verification call).
